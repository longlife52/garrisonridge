Garrison Ridge is a collection of creative talent led by Producer/Writer, Lisa Long. Since 1995, Garrison Ridge has created award-winning television and video programs as well as print and web products.

From managing the creation of the NFL's Jacksonville Jaguars Broadcasting and Creative Services Department to a nationally recognized children's newsmagazine and long-form documentaries, Lisa tells inspiring stories about real people.

In addition, from wellness, fitness, and healthcare to the transportation industry, through online forums and live events, Lisa creates, motivates, celebrates, and facilitates positive change.
